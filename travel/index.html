Local Transportation

Free parking is available at Ste. Anne de Detroit, and parking passes are available for the Colony Club. Please see the Venues page for more detailed parking information. Parking at the Hilton Garden Inn is by valet only.

A shuttle will be available between the Hilton Garden Inn and the Colonly Club immediately prior to and during the reception. This page has more detailed shuttle information.

Additionally, Uber and Lyft are both available in Downtown Detroit.

Flying

We suggest out-of-town guests fly into DTW (Detroit Metropolitan Wayne County Airport). There are multiple taxi services that can bring you into the city, such as Metro Cars and Uber. If you instead opt for a rental car, the drive from DTW to Detroit is approximately 25 minutes.
