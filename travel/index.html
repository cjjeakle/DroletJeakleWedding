Flying

We suggest out-of-town guests fly into DTW (Detroit Metropolitan Wayne County Airport). There are multiple taxi services that can bring you into the city, such as Metro Cars and Uber. If you instead opt for a rental car, the drive from DTW to Detroit is approximately 25 minutes.

Driving

If you are driving to the wedding, several major highways can bring you into the city, including I-75, M-10, and I-96. Please be mindful of any construction and prepare for possible delays.

Accommodations

It is important to note is that there is going to be a large, city-wide event the same weekend as our wedding. Hotel rooms will sell fast. If you do intend on coming (and we’re hoping you do!), please reserve your room as soon as possible.
